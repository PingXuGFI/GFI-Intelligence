What GL Measures

Capital velocity, structurally verified.

資本速度，結構性驗證。

01 · Fs

Flow Success Rate

% of eligible processes completing core workflow without friction failure.

02 · Vn

Strategic Value

Weighted policy and capital impact scored 0–10 against objectives.

03 · Pd

Pain Duration

Annual hours consumed by structural friction — approvals, rework loops.

04 · Cf

Cognitive Friction

Mental load index (0–10) reflecting complexity imposed on users.

GL = (Fs × Vn) / (Pd × Cf)·GLr = (Fs × Vn) / (Pd × Cf × SRF)
