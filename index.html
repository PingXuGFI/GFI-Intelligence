What This Is

Capital Efficiency Verification is a new category of independent structural analysis. It does not design transformation. It does not implement automation. It verifies whether capital efficiency improved as a result of change.

How It Works

Baseline Measurement

Measure capital velocity and structural load before transformation.

Post-Change Verification

Quantify efficiency shift after automation, ERP, AI, or restructuring.

Efficiency Delta

Identify whether capital efficiency increased or deteriorated.

Why It Matters

Automation success does not guarantee capital efficiency gain. GL verifies outcomes, not deployment narratives. This creates an independent layer of capital performance validation.
